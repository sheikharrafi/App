Quantum communication has moved from physics papers to working prototypes. Research teams are already testing **quantum repeaters**, **entanglement distribution**, and secure links over metropolitan distances.

## Why it matters

A quantum internet could enable ultra-secure messaging and distributed quantum computing. Instead of replacing the internet, it will likely work as a specialized layer for security-critical systems.

## What is still missing

Engineers are still solving reliability and scale. But the progress curve suggests the first practical networks may arrive faster than expected.
