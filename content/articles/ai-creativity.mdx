Modern AI can produce music, text, and design ideas that feel surprisingly original. But creativity is not only output quality—it is also intent, taste, and context.

## The creative partnership model

In many workflows, AI acts as a **co-creator**: it proposes variants, while humans evaluate meaning and direction.

## Bottom line

Machines can support and extend creativity, but human judgment still drives what is truly valuable.
